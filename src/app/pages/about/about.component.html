About

This sample app features both sides of typical food ordering application: client's side and admins dashboard. Links to FrontEnd and BackEnd source codes can be found in the footer.

From the client's side users can:

  • Authorize.
  • Add dishes to the shopping cart.
  • Create orders for dishes in the cart.

In the admins dashboard users have ability to:

  • Create, edit or delete categories and dishes.
  • See the list of users and their roles.
  • See the list of orders and their contents. Change orders statuses.

Accessing Admins Dashboard

To access the dashboard, follow these simple steps:

  • Click on login button in the top right corner.
  • In the popped up dialog, click on Sign in as Admin button.
  • Navigate to 'Dashboard' from the top nav.

Features

  • Responsive design.
  • Lazy-loaded modules.
  • No hardcoded data. Everything comes through AJAX.
  • Image upload with instant preview.
  • Locally preserving shopping cart state.
  • Guarded routes.
